Rationale

The purpose of Utour is to enhance global support in increasing efforts in stop poaching and the trafficking of protected species. In order to do this more is needed to be done in giving local communities sustainable and respectable job opportunities. Utour gives locals a chance to earn a respectable income. They get to be their own boss and set their own hours. Utour connects these locals with tourist in the area.
