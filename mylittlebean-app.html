Users are pregnant mothers who need help managing a healthy and safe pregnancy.

My little bean is a pregnancy monitoring app that connects to a wearable belt. Users will receive 24/7 updates on the progression and health of their baby and the mother. It monitors the baby's heartbeat, position, visual defects and progression. While also monitoring the mother's blood pressure, heart rate and basic health. User can also pass on this information to physicians and loved ones.

User Story/Flow

User story 1

Users want an easy way to get answers they have about their pregnancy.

User story 2

Users want to send health and progression updates on how both mom and baby to doctors and loved ones.

User story 3

Users want to receive 24/7 updates on the progression and health of baby and the mother.

Visual Design

Rationale

Being pregnant is a busy and stressful time.The My Little Bean app takes off some of that stress by constantly giving moms updates on their babies’ health and progression. Being pregnant is a family affair. My Little Bean app allows users to send updates of both the mother and baby’s health to friends, family and doctors.
