Concepts

The concept for this annual report was to keep it very graphic based. I wanted the report to be easy to understand and what been way to do that then infographics. Having a heavily graphic look supports the informative look of the report.

Style

The style of this report is graphic and science based. The science elements were important as it reflects the company and their work in a science based industry. Consistent circle pattern that resemble cells were used as a recurring pattern throughout report. Each section has graphic elements that explain the information on the page. The colours are bright and bold. This gives the booklet a positive and uplifting feel and reflects the good work the research institute does.
