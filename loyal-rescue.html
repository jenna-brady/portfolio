Loyal rescue

Based in Ontario, Loyal Rescue is a non-profit, privately run, foster-based dog rescue.

Target audience

Target audience are animals lovers and people who are interested in adopting or fostering a dog. They are are caring and patient individuals. These people are employed adults, who have a enough time in their life to take care of a dog.

The Process

Website Goal

I created a pattern library for Rescue Loyal and three pages of the website. Loyal Rescue wants to provide the public with an opportunity to adopt dogs who are homeless or may have came from an abusive home. While also letting the public know about future events and give them a opportunity to donate.
