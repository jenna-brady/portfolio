Who is The Red Fox

Have been a family owned restaurant since 1958. Residing in the historic town of Perth, The Red Fox serves top of the line pub style foods. They pride themselves in their small town atmosphere. Their values are founded on tradition and community. The Red Fox is a place you can go after a hard day at work and catch up with old friends.

Target audience

The Red Fox attract males and females between the ages of 30 and 50. These people are middle to lower class with a stable income. Most of the regular customers are locals from Perth. They enjoy sports and tradition.
